# SORA STUDIO — Web Design Parameters
### Brand Design System & Reference Guide

---

## Brand Essence

**One-liner:** A clean, high-fashion, premium beauty brand with a minimalist monochrome identity, authentic editorial photography, and slow cinematic motion that feels elegant, organized, and subtly elite.

**Keywords:** Clean · Premium · Minimalist · Monochrome · Editorial · Elegant · Organized · Subtly Elite

---

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Palette (Monochrome Core)

| Role | Hex | Name | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Background | `#FFFFFF` | Pure White | Primary background, hero sections, product cards |
| Background Alt | `#F5F3F0` | Warm Off-White | Section alternation, content blocks |
| Surface | `#E8E4DF` | Warm Light Grey | Cards, containers, dividers |
| Surface Deep | `#B8B2AA` | Warm Mid Grey | Borders, subtle separators |
| Text Primary | `#1A1816` | Near Black | Headlines, body copy |
| Text Secondary | `#6B665F` | Warm Grey | Captions, metadata, secondary info |
| Text Tertiary | `#9E9890` | Light Warm Grey | Placeholders, disabled text |

### Accent Palette (Reflective & Luxe)

| Role | Hex | Name | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gold Accent | `#C4A76C` | Muted Gold | CTA buttons, hover highlights, "Add to Cart" |
| Gold Light | `#DBC9A0` | Soft Gold | Icon tints, badge backgrounds |
| Pearl Shimmer | `#EDE8E0` | Pearl | Hover states, card glows, shimmer overlays |
| Rose Nude | `#D4B5A0` | Warm Nude | Product category tags, soft accents |
| Silver | `#C0C0C0` | Silver | Secondary metallic accents, icons |

### Semantic Colors

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Success | `#7A9E6B` | Order confirmed, in-stock indicators |
| Warning | `#C4976C` | Low stock, shipping notice |
| Error | `#A65D5D` | Form errors, out-of-stock (muted, not alarming) |

### Color Rules

- **Dominant split:** 70% white/off-white · 20% warm greys · 10% gold/metallic accents
- **Never use:** Saturated neon, bright primary colors, cool-toned greys (blue-grey)
- **Hover states:** Shift towards gold or pearl shimmer — never darken aggressively
- **Dark mode (optional):** `#1A1816` background, `#F5F3F0` text, gold accent stays the same
- **Contrast check:** All text combinations must pass WCAG AA (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large)

## 2. Typography

### Font Selection

| Role | Primary Font | Fallback | Weight | Style |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Logo / Brand | Cormorant Garamond | Georgia | 300–500 | Uppercase, wide letter-spacing |
| Headlines (H1–H2) | Cormorant Garamond | Georgia | 400–600 | Sentence case or uppercase |
| Subheadings (H3–H4) | Sora (Google Font) | Inter, sans-serif | 400–500 | Sentence case |
| Body Text | Sora | Inter, sans-serif | 300–400 | Regular |
| Captions / Labels | Sora | Inter, sans-serif | 300 | Uppercase with tracking |
| Buttons / CTAs | Sora | Inter, sans-serif | 500 | Uppercase, letter-spaced |

> **Why Cormorant Garamond:** High-contrast serif with elegant thin strokes — reads editorial and luxury without being overused. Free on Google Fonts/Fontshare.
> **Why Sora:** The brand is literally called "Sora" — using the Sora typeface (Google Fonts) for body copy creates a subtle, clever brand-typography connection. Clean, geometric, modern.

### Type Scale (Desktop → Mobile)

| Element | Desktop | Tablet | Mobile | Line Height | Letter Spacing |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hero Title | 72–96px | 56–64px | 40–48px | 1.05 | +0.08em |
| H1 | 48–56px | 40–44px | 32–36px | 1.1 | +0.04em |
| H2 | 36–40px | 32–36px | 28–32px | 1.15 | +0.02em |
| H3 | 24–28px | 22–24px | 20–22px | 1.25 | +0.01em |
| Body Large | 18–20px | 18px | 16–18px | 1.6 | 0 |
| Body | 16px | 16px | 15–16px | 1.6 | 0 |
| Caption | 12–13px | 12px | 11–12px | 1.4 | +0.06em |
| Button | 13–14px | 13px | 13px | 1 | +0.12em |

### Typography Rules

- **Measure:** 55–70 characters per line for body text
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned or center-aligned (for hero sections only)
- **Uppercase usage:** Logo, buttons, captions, navigation links — never for body paragraphs
- **Never use:** Bold body text for emphasis (use Medium weight instead), underlines for non-links
- **Responsive:** Type scale drops ~20–25% from desktop to mobile

## 3. Spacing & Layout

### Spacing Scale (8px Base Grid)

| Token | Value | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| `space-xs` | 4px | Inline icon gaps |
| `space-sm` | 8px | Tight element gaps |
| `space-md` | 16px | Card internal padding |
| `space-lg` | 24px | Between related sections |
| `space-xl` | 48px | Between major sections |
| `space-2xl` | 80px | Hero section padding |
| `space-3xl` | 120px | Page section separation |

### Layout Grid

| Breakpoint | Columns | Gutter | Margin |
|---|---|---|---|
| Desktop (1440px) | 12 | 24px | 80–120px |
| Tablet (768px) | 8 | 20px | 40–48px |
| Mobile (375px) | 4 | 16px | 20–24px |

### White Space Philosophy

- **Generous negative space** is the brand signature — it communicates luxury and calm
- **Hero sections:** Min 40vh height, often full-viewport with a single image + minimal text
- **Product grids:** 2–3 columns on desktop (never 4+ — too crowded for premium feel)
- **Between sections:** At least 80–120px breathing room
- **Content max-width:** 1200px for text-heavy, 1440px for full-bleed visuals
- **Rule:** If it feels "almost too much space," it's right for this brand

## 4. Imagery Style

### Photography Direction

| Parameter | Specification |
|---|---|
| Type | Authentic, realistic editorial photography — never AI-generated or stock |
| Lighting | Warm studio lighting, soft directional light, natural window light |
| Color Grade | Desaturated warm tones, slight lift in shadows, warm highlights |
| Resolution | Min 2x retina (e.g., 1200px display = 2400px source) |
| Aspect Ratios | 3:4 (product portraits), 1:1 (grid), 16:9 (hero banners), 4:5 (mobile hero) |

### Model Direction

| Parameter | Specification |
|---|---|
| Age Range | 20–30 years old |
| Demographic | Asian, socialite aesthetic |
| Posture | Confident, bold — "berani" energy, editorial stance |
| Facial Expression | 70% soft resting natural face, 20% light smile, 10% editorial gaze |
| Eye Shape Reference | Almond / slightly narrow (mata sipit) — natural beauty, no heavy makeup |
| Hand Gestures | Holding coffee cup, touching sunglasses, gentle hand-near-face poses |
| Styling | Minimal jewelry — pearl earrings, silver/gold rings, delicate chains |

### Product Shot Styling

| Parameter | Specification |
|---|---|
| Accessories | Pearl, silver, gold elements |
| Reflective Elements | Play with glass, mirror, chrome — surfaces that catch light |
| Background | White, light grey, monochrome studio, glass surfaces |
| Composition | Large-scale close-ups for drama; product hero shots fill 60–80% of frame |
| Props | White towels with eucalyptus, blush-toned nail polish bottles, cream textures |

### Context / Scene Settings

- Catwalk / runway reference
- Hand gesture holding a coffee cup (café editorial)
- Sunglasses gesture (to-go, street style)
- High fashion magazine editorial spread feel

## 5. Visual Hierarchy

### Priority Order

| Level | What | How |
|---|---|---|
| **Hierarchy 1** | Hero image / product visual | Placement (top/center), size (dominant), color (warm tones against white) |
| **Hierarchy 2** | Key benefit text + brand logo | Medium size, gold or dark text, positioned below or overlaid on image |
| **Hierarchy 3** | Supporting copy / description | Smaller body text, warm grey color |
| **Hierarchy 4** | Navigation / metadata | Smallest, lightest grey, minimal visual weight |

### Composition Principles

- **Organized and clean** — everything aligned to grid, no randomness
- **Gestalt grouping:** Related items proximity-grouped; clear separation between sections
- **Negative space** as a design element (not empty — intentional)
- **Asymmetric balance:** Hero image on one side, text on the other (editorial layout)
- **Consistent cadence:** Alternate between image-heavy and text sections rhythmically

## 6. Shadows, Depth & Texture

### Shadow System

| Level | CSS Value | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Elevation 0 | none | Flat cards, default state |
| Elevation 1 | `0 2px 8px rgba(26,24,22,0.04)` | Cards on hover, subtle lift |
| Elevation 2 | `0 4px 16px rgba(26,24,22,0.06)` | Dropdowns, modals |
| Elevation 3 | `0 8px 32px rgba(26,24,22,0.08)` | Hero overlays, floating elements |

### Rules

- **Soft shadows only** — no hard drop shadows
- **Warm-toned shadows** (brown-black, not blue-black)
- **Texture:** Soft, smooth — references to towel fabric, smooth packaging, glass surfaces
- **No harsh borders** — use shadow or subtle tone shifts to separate elements
- **Glass/frosted effects:** Allowed sparingly for overlays (backdrop-filter: blur)

## 7. Motion & Animation

### Principles

- **Slow, deliberate, cinematic** — never fast or flashy
- **Mimics breathing rhythm** — ease-in-out with longer durations
- **Monochrome or soft tones** maintained throughout — no color burst transitions

### Timing Tokens

| Token | Duration | Easing | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| `motion-subtle` | 300ms | ease-in-out | Hover states, opacity shifts |
| `motion-standard` | 600ms | cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1) | Page transitions, card reveals |
| `motion-slow` | 1000–1500ms | cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) | Hero animations, logo fade-in |
| `motion-cinematic` | 2000–3000ms | ease-in-out | Background parallax, scene transitions |

### Allowed Animations

| Animation | Specification |
|---|---|
| Logo entrance | Gentle fade-in (1.5s), slight scale from 0.96 → 1 |
| Text entrance | Slide up 12–20px + fade in (600–800ms), staggered per line (100ms delay) |
| Product reveal | Fade in + subtle scale (0.98 → 1), 800ms |
| Hover — cards | Soft shadow increase + slight lift (translateY -2px), 300ms |
| Hover — images | Slow gentle zoom (scale 1 → 1.03), 600ms |
| Scroll parallax | Background moves at 0.3x scroll speed — subtle, not dramatic |
| Pearl/shimmer | CSS shimmer gradient sweep, 3–4s loop, very low opacity (0.05–0.1) |
| Page transition | Gentle fade (400ms out, 600ms in) — not slide or flip |

### Prohibited

- Fast cuts, bounce effects, elastic easing
- Spinning, flipping, 3D rotations
- Typing effects, counter animations
- Anything that breaks the "calm breathing" rhythm

### Video / Motion Content on Site

- Main frame = still editorial photo
- One small moving element only (light reflection on pearl, floating tag, tiny shimmer trail)
- Background video if used: slow camera glide, desaturated, low opacity overlay
- `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` — disable all animation, show static versions

## 8. UI Component Style

### Buttons

| Type | Style |
|---|---|
| Primary CTA | Background `#C4A76C` (gold), text `#FFFFFF`, uppercase, letter-spaced, rounded-sm (4px), padding 14px 32px |
| Secondary | Border 1px `#1A1816`, transparent bg, text `#1A1816`, same shape |
| Ghost | No border, text `#6B665F`, underline on hover (thin, gold) |
| Hover | Gold shifts to `#A88B50` (darker gold), 300ms ease |

### Input Fields

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Border | 1px `#E8E4DF` bottom-only (underline style) |
| Focus | Border shifts to `#C4A76C` gold |
| Background | Transparent |
| Text | `#1A1816`, Sora 16px |
| Label | `#9E9890`, Sora 12px uppercase, letter-spaced |

### Navigation

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Style | Minimal top bar, logo centered, links flanked |
| Font | Sora 13px, uppercase, letter-spacing +0.1em |
| Color | `#1A1816` default, `#C4A76C` on hover/active |
| Mobile | Full-screen overlay menu, fade-in, centered links large type |
| Sticky | On scroll: background fades to `#FFFFFF` with soft shadow |

### Cards (Product)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Border | None (shadow on hover) |
| Border radius | 0px or 2px (sharp and editorial) |
| Image | 3:4 ratio, soft zoom on hover (scale 1.03) |
| Title | Sora 14px medium, `#1A1816` |
| Price | Sora 13px light, `#6B665F` |
| Spacing | 16px internal padding, 24px between cards |

## 9. Sensory Translation (Digital ↔ Physical)

These sensory cues guide the overall UX "feel" — even on a screen.

| Sense | Physical Reference | Web Translation |
|---|---|---|
| **Sight** | Warm studio, monochrome, glass surfaces | Soft warm palette, generous white space, shimmer micro-animations |
| **Hear** | Calm salon music, satisfying product sounds | Optional ambient audio on landing (muted default), soft UI click sounds |
| **Smell** | Clean floral signature scent | Imagery of eucalyptus, white flowers, clean spa environments — evoke through visuals |
| **Taste** | Small welcome drink or candy | "Treat" micro-interactions — unexpected delightful hover effects, a small animation reward |
| **Touch** | Soft towels, smooth packaging, luxe texture | Smooth scroll, soft shadows, rounded hover states, "tactile" button press animations (slight depress) |

## 11. Responsive Behavior

| Behavior | Desktop | Mobile |
|---|---|---|
| Navigation | Horizontal bar with centered logo | Hamburger → full-screen overlay |
| Hero | Full bleed image + overlaid text | Stacked: image above, text below |
| Product grid | 2–3 columns | 1–2 columns |

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|

| Blacks | +5 (no true black — keeps it gentle) |
| Saturation | -15 to -20 (desaturated, editorial) |
| Vibrance | -5 |
| Grain | None or minimal |

## 13. Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- Use generous white space — luxury brands breathe
- Keep everything aligned to the grid
- Use warm tones only (beige, cream, warm grey, gold)
- Show real, editorial-quality photography
- Animate slowly and deliberately
- Maintain consistency across every page

### Don't

- Use bright, saturated, or neon colors
- Crowd content — no cluttered grids or walls of text
- Use stock photography or obviously AI-generated faces
- Animate quickly or with bounce/elastic effects
- Use more than 2 font families
- Break the monochrome + gold accent rule
- Use cool-toned greys or blues anywhere
